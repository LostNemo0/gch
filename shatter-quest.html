How to Shatter/Harmonize Crystal Quest

Shattering Crystal requires 3 different kind of crystal. You need 5 pieces of it for crytal harmonization.
For this tutorial I used Celestial Frame.
You can also check the other combination at the forum.

As you can see there are different kinds of amount you needed.
Like vastly more, far more, slightly more to increase the Chi

note: vastly more means high rarity(40+), far more means not equal to the first block you put, rarity(25-40), more(rarity 15-25), and slightly more means low rarity(1-15),


Also there are different kinds of amount you needed to decrease the Chi
Like vastly less, far less, slightly less

note: vastly less means high rarity(40+), far less means not equal to the first block you put, rarity(25-40), less(rarity 15-25), and slightly less means low rarity(1-15)


You can watch the full tutorial here :)


After harmonizing the Crystal you can now Tap it to break the crystal and get the item.
Now you are done with your Shatter Quest :)
